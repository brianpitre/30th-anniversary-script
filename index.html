Opening

Intro — Voice Over

With Music & Clips  ·  2 Min
Open with Leslie and Pastor John walking into church… Meeting up with the team… Praying before we walk into service — 30 Seconds
Pastor John — Voice Over
Maybe the most common question I get about The Life Church is, "Did I see all of this when we were just starting out 30 years ago?" I'm never sure exactly what they mean when they say ALL OF THIS. Maybe they're talking about the Sunday Service crowds, or the Conferences, or maybe it's the Global Footprint we now have with locations stretching from Memphis to New York to Italy. And places in between. Maybe they're talking about the amazing team that we have.

Did I see it? Of course, the answer is, "No!"

If you could have seen and felt what it was like 30 years ago… Oh man… There was so much that you would have had to dig through to find the potential. But it was there. The heart was there and the vision and the hard work… No, I didn't really see all of this back then, but I always felt, even from the beginning, that God was going to do something significant.

Now, here we are 30 years later and Leslie and I are amazed.

Timecode mark: 1:30
